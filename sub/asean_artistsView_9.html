Gam Wichayanee - Mai Mee Tur Mai Tai(I’m fine without you)

Thailand

Gam Wichayanee

Gam Wichayanee is the first female winner of "The Star" singing contest 2008 from Thailand and was only 18 years old while she competed. She obtained overwhelming votes from the public because of her outstanding performance.

With her powerful singing techniques and emotional performance, now supported by young generation music fans as the most powerful female singer.

She is able to sing in many genres, from pop, R&B, jazz in many languages, including Thai, English, and Korean.
