sunwoojunga - Run With Me

KOREA

sunwoojunga

Sunwoojunga freely recreates herself regardless of genres such as rock, jazz, pop, R&B, hip-hop and electronic, showing her individuality through it.

As an outstanding vocalist, she handles her voice skillfully to maximize the original taste of the music while creating waves with her unstructured freewheeling style.

Sunwoojunga, who plays various roles such as producing, music director, and singer-songwriter, is also called the "Musician's Favorite Musician" that artists want to collaborate the most.
