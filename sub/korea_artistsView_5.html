JAMIE - Apollo 11

KOREA

JAMIE

Jamie, who was loved by the public for her natural rhythm and explosive singing skills from her first appearance on the audition program, made her debut with Baek Ye-rin and duo '15&'.

She later showed her growth as a solo artist.

Recently, Jamie once again showed a strong presence to the public through various activities, and showed an original musical spectrum through her new song 'Apollo 11.'
