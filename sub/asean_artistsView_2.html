SmallWorld SmallBand - 2x5

Cambodia

SMALLWORLD SMALLBAND

SmallWorld SmallBand is a contemporary musician who creates incredible storytelling music with hopes to redefine the Cambodian sound.

SmallWorld SmallBand has put a big move on their first debut album, ‘2×5’. The band came together and first entered the national spotlight five years ago with their hit original song ‘Khmer Flag’. From there, they have amassed a youthful fan base.
