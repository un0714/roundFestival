george - Boat

KOREA

george

George released his first single 'IAMGOERGE' in 2016, and then released the single 'Boat' in his current label.

With George's comfortable yet unique voice and cheerful music video, 'Boat' instantly became an opportunity for the public to know him.

In the next two years, He released single 'Swimming Pool', 'look at me' and EP albums 'Cassette' and 'LEEEE', which showed his wide musical spectrum. Currently, George works with artists of various genres and OSTs.

In 2018, his first EP album 'Cassette' was nominated for the Korean Popular Music Award.
