DAYBREAK - Flower Road

KOREA

DAYBREAK

DAYBREAK is based on Pop music under the motto of "Free in Music", making music of various genres, including Rock, Jazz, and City Pop into their own style.

They started their career with their debut album [Urban Life Style] in 2007, drawing attention for their representative songs such as 'Good' and 'Love Actually'. Later, they won music contest programs and showed good performances to the public through TV broadcasts.

They are called the national band of Korea, receiving great love for their title track 'Flower Road' from their fourth full-length album [WITH] released in 2016.
