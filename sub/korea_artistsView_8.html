SORAN

SORAN, a four-member band pursuing modern rock, is loved for its sophisticated and lovely lyrics and pop songs that sing daily life in which comfort and wit coexist.

They have sold out a series of their own brand concerts that have broken the framework of the existing performances, showing an unrivaled presence in the Korean music scene.

Guitarist Lee Tae wook has participated in the albums of top artists such as BTS, and vocalist Ko Young bae is drawing attention in various broadcasting programs as the next generation of entertainers and MCs, and all members are now in an important position in the Korean music scene.
