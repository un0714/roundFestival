ROUND Music Forum

ROUND Forum covers music business issues in Korea and ASEAN countries. Most of us present in our own native languages, but we will make it easier to understand each other's stories by providing English subtitles. I hope we can use these short sessions to Re:connect each other.

Topic #1:

How to connect the world with music - Let’s learn about global music distribution.

  • - How do artists and labels release music globally
  • - What really happens to your music when you release it to the world
  • - Things to keep in mind before going global.

  • SPEAKER : S.Korea Jeffrey Chaing (Global Business for Fluxus)
