Dila Junaidi - Aku Milik Orang (I’m Owned)

BRUNEI

Dila Junaidi

Dila Junaidi, singer songwriter had a debut at Bintang Kecil in 2003 as the first runner up.

Since then, she participated in several audition programs and took 2nd place. Since then, she released her first single titled “Aku Milik Orang” in 2009 and performed in not only Brunei but also various countries. Recently, released her fifth single, titled “Rasa Untuk Setia” (A duet with Rizal Rasid) in October 2019 and won 'Best Music Video' for 'Rasa Ini Cinta' in Anugerah Pelangi Ke-7.
