Ben&Ben - Ride Home

Philippines

Ben&Ben

Their debut album 'Limasawa Street' was released with both critical and commercial success. In a short span of time, Ben&Ben quickly rose to the top ranks of the Philippine music industry as currently the most streamed Filipino artists, and one of the most successful artists in the Asian region.

Their eclectic foray into folk and world music blending seamlessly with intricate pop sensibilities.

Their songs paint an indelible portrait of a generation that knows their worth despite the brokenness inside, creating a connection between the personal and the universal.
