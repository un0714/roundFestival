BRUNEI

MD ABU SOFFIAN BIN MD ALINOORDIN

Mr. Md Abu Soffian Bin Md Alinoordin is a music conductor, composer, and music arranger. He organizes activities such as workshops, competitions and programs for music development specifically traditional music. He conducts research specifically in Brunei traditional music, and participated in performances conducted in house, national, regional and international as well.

CAMBODIA

SOPHY KEO

Mr.Sophy Keo has composed and arranged not only many songs but also music for film for the Royal Palace Film. Besides, he has composed many pieces for piano, duo, trio, quartet chamber and symphony orchestra. Composed and arranged for traditional with western instruments. Since 2017, he has been acting as vice dean of faculty of music Royal University of Fine Arts. He is endeavouring to revive traditional Cambodian music by cultivating enthusiastic and talented new artists.

INDONESIA

AZFANSADRA KARIM

Azfansadra Karim played with a variety of musicians, big bands and music projects worldwide. He has toured around Europe and Japan with different projects, and currently working actively as arranger, composer, producer and a keyboard player. Released albums together with Tomorrow People Ensemble, Zatua and also appeared in several recordings as a sideman both abroad and in Indonesia.

Lao PDR

DOUANGCHAMPY VOUTHISOUK

Douangchampy Vouthisouk is Deputy-Director General of Performing Arts Ministry of Information, Culture and Tourism. He is putting a lot of effort into research and thesis for the Lao music industry. Besides, he collected and provided regarding Lao traditional music and folksong in 2001 for ASEAN Project in Singapore.

MALAYSIA

NASRAN BIN NAWI

Nasran Bin Nawi has been engaged as a secessionist for the concert performances of Sarah Brightman, Il Divo, and Richard Clayderman. His international engagements were with the Asia Pacific String Ensemble, with performances in Tokyo, Nagoya and the ASEAN Japan Festival Orchestra in Bangkok. He was an active cello tutor at the National Arts Academy, and UCSI University. Currently, he is the cello principal of the National Symphony Orchestra (NSO) of Malaysia.

MYANMAR

SU ZAR ZAR HTAY YEE

Dr. Su Zar Zar Htay Yee became the first Ph.D music holder in Myanmar and assistant lecturer of Music Department, National University of Arts and Culture (Yangon). She is currently teaching Myanmar Traditional Harp (practical) and Aesthetic (lecture) and one of the most hard working teachers and skillful teachers of National University of Arts and Culture (Yangon). Dr.Su got her Ph.D in Music from Tokyo University of the Arts in 2015, and is a fluent speaker of Japanese and English.

PHILIPPINES

ROSELLER JUAN MIGUEL L. CONSTANTINO

Mike is still DJs professionally. More recently, he headed Brand New Media, a global content marketing agency, was the Country Sales Director of Yahoo Philippines and was the Marketing Head for Asia Pacific for The Filipino Channel. In 2019, together with the National Commission for Culture & The Arts, Mike founded Sonik Philippines, the first Philippine-centric International Music Conference x Festival in the country and now annually.

SINGAPORE

LAWRENCE QUEK

Lawrence is based in Singapore with over 8 years of experience in curating programmes, music business development, and A&R. Currently a music programmer at the Esplanade, focuses on music festivals and series, such as Baybeats, Rocking The Region, Mosaic Music Series featuring Korean artists and other extraordinary musicians from all over the world. He had worked in Universal Music Singapore, identifying potential Singaporean artists to be signed to the label, and creating business opportunities for the artists.

THAILAND

JAKKAWAL SAOTHONGYUTTITUM

He is a composer and music producer who has worked with many famous artists in Thailand. In addition, he worked as a music director at GMM GRAMMY in Thailand. As director and producer, he has been involved in various music broadcasting programs in Thailand such as "The Mask Singer", "The Golden Song" and etc.Besides, he participated in various charity concerts and wrote music for charity and campaigns. He is also an active lecturer at universities, corporations, and etc.

VIET NAM

QUOC TRUNG

Quoc Trung is a music producer and composer, also known as the founder and festival director of Monsoon Music Festival. With almost 20 years of working in the music industry, he has got many chances to work on music projects, including composing, producing, mixing, mastering, arranging, with different artists and different producers/ composers/partners in Vietnam as well as in foreign countries.

S.KOREA

HYUN CHUL KIM

Kim Hyun chul, who debuted in 1989, is one the best producers and singer-songwriter in Korea. Through the genre of city-pop, he is called the "City-pop Godfather," presenting a sophisticated and sensuously melody. Recently, his debut album title track 'It's Been A While', called 'Hidden Masterpiece' among music fans, has been released in a new style in 31 years, showing steady music moves.
