KOREA

Elaine

Elaine is a singer-songwriter who shows charming vocals, deep musical expressions and delicate sensibilities, and once you hear her voice, you can't help but fall in love.

She is called Damien Rice in Korea, beginning to promote herself through various music contest programs.

With the emotional voice that boosts the emotions of the play, she is definitely making an unrivaled move by participating in many drama OSTs, including 'Mr. Sunshine', 'Memories of the Alhambra', 'It's Okay to Not Be Okay', 'Vagabond' etc.
